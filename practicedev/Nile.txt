Nile University of Nigeria (NILE) is a private university located in Abuja, Nigeria. The University is Ranked the 5th Best University in Nigeria Which Makes it The Best Private University in Nigeria.The University was established in 2009.The University Administration is Under The Royal Family of The United Kingdom.At the time of formation, NILE had three faculties (Arts & Social Sciences, Engineering, and Natural & Applied Sciences) and 93 students. Currently, Nile University of Nigeria has six faculties and a School of Postgraduate Studies, as well as over 4,000 students (undergraduate and postgraduate). NILE currently offers 33 undergraduate programs and 43 postgraduate programs.
